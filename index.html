Steganography Web Page

Hide one image in another image and generate original images back.

    Steps to be followed:
  1. Upload two images and press on 'Hide Image'.
    • A new steganographed image is generated which looks similar to 'Input' image.
  2. Press 'Show Image' button.
    • It will regenerate both original images from that steganographed image.
